Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the goal of developing this website?

    Our website aims to educate and inspire individuals to reduce their digital footprint by providing practical tools, guides, quizzes, and easy-to-follow tips for all ages. We offer resources that encourage more sustainable digital habits which contribute to a greener future.
  • History of the website?

    We created this website on April 22, 2025, with the vision of promoting digital sustainability and encouraging individuals to make eco-conscious choices in their online and tech activities in an easy and accessible way. Initially, the website started as a project to raise awareness about the environmental impact of digital habits under the broader topic of sustainable living. Over time, it evolved with the incorporation of creative ideas and innovative designs from our developers, making it a comprehensive platform for fostering digital sustainability.
  • Why do we make this website?

    We created this website to address the growing environmental concerns surrounding digital technology and its impact on the planet. As technology continues to develop and expand, so does our carbon footprint and waste generation. By creating this website, we aim to provide accessible resources and tools that help people make informed and responsible choices, allowing them to reduce their digital impact without sacrificing convenience or enjoyment.
  • What benefits are we expecting from our website?

    We aim to provide users with the knowledge and activities that will help reduce their environmental impact through mindful digital choices. The benefits we seek from this website include fostering a community of eco-conscious users, raising awareness about digital sustainability, encouraging responsible consumption, and achieving measurable reductions in digital waste and energy usage.
  • Our future plans and goals?

    Our future plans include expanding our content and features, such as vlogs, a news channel, personalized tools, partnerships with eco-friendly tech brands, and community-driven challenges/campaigns to make a meaningful impact while keeping it fun. Once we perfect the website, we plan to launch a mobile app to help users learn more about their digital footprint, stay updated on the latest sustainable tech trends, and continue making eco-conscious choices on the go.

The vlog: How to practice digital sustainability?

The purpose of this video is to promote digital sustainability by showing how small changes in our online habits can make a big environmental impact. As we rely more on digital devices, cloud storage, and internet use, we often overlook the hidden energy costs behind them ‚Äî such as the electricity used by data centers and networks. This video aims to:

  • Teach simple ways to save energy online especially for students and everyday digital users.

  • Show real-life actions like deleting emails, streaming in SD, compressing files, and using energy-saving settings.

  • Promote responsible digital habits that save power and support a greener future.

  • Inspire small changes that add up to a big impact, like cleaning storage or shutting off devices.
