Think learning about sustainability is boring? Think again! This quiz isn’t just about facts — it’s a fun way to test your eco-smarts while picking up cool tips on how to help the planet. Whether you’re a recycling rookie or a green genius, there’s something for everyone. So don’t just scroll by — take the quiz, don’t delay, save the Earth the fun way! Every answer gets you one step closer to living a more sustainable life (and hey, bragging rights never hurt).

🌱 Sustainability Quiz Grading Scale (Out of 10 Marks)

🔴 0 – 4 marks: Earth Explorer

Just getting started! It’s okay — everyone begins somewhere. This is your chance to grow your knowledge and become more planet-wise. Give it another shot! 🌱 Grade: D or below

🟡 5 – 7 marks: Green Learner

You're on your way! You know the basics but there’s still more to learn. With a bit more effort, you’ll be a full-on eco champ in no time. Keep going! 🍃 Grade: B to C

🟢 8 – 10 marks: Eco Expert

You know your stuff! You've got a strong grip on sustainability and how to live green. You’re ready to take action and even teach others. Mother Earth is proud of you! 🌍💚 Grade: A
